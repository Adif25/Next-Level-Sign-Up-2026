About the Conference

Built to grow bold leaders.

Next Level is the City of Wilmington Parks & Recreation's youth leadership conference — a day where young people discover their strengths, find their voice, and connect with a community that believes in them.

Our Mission

Every young person deserves a launchpad.

We created Next Level because leadership isn't something you're born with — it's something you build. Through workshops, mentorship, and real connection, we give Wilmington's youth the tools and confidence to lead in their schools, their neighborhoods, and their own lives.

For one powerful day, our young people step into a space designed entirely for their growth — and walk out ready for what's next.
